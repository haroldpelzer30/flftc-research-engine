# Key Findings

## Observation 1

Inside Bars dramatically reduced opportunity count.

| Test | Signals |
|---------|---------:|
| BB Alone | 5,274 |
| BB + Inside Bar | 556 |

Opportunity reduction:

**~89.5%**

---

## Observation 2

The strongest result occurred immediately after the signal.

Bullish Side:

* 56.20% after 1 candle
* Edge largely disappeared afterward

---

## Observation 3

* 50.34%
* 47.32%
* 52.35%

# Preliminary Conclusion

Inside Bars improved Bollinger Band performance substantially compared to BB Alone.

However:

* The improvement came at the cost of nearly 90% of opportunities.
* Most of the edge occurred immediately after the signal.
* Follow-through remained weak.

---

# Research Takeaway

Inside Bars appear to function as a powerful trade filter, but much like previous studies, the increase in win rate comes with a dramatic reduction in opportunity count.

The central question remains:

> Does the improvement justify the destruction of nearly 90% of potential trades?

At this stage, Daily Structure remains the strongest Bollinger Band filter tested on EURUSD.
