# Preliminary Conclusion

Double Bottom / Top appears to improve Bollinger Band signals modestly.

However, the improvement is significantly smaller than the improvement produced by Daily Structure.

The evidence currently suggests:

* Daily Structure remains the strongest Bollinger Band filter tested.
* Double Bottom / Top improves performance but does not create a strong edge.
* Most of the predictive value may be coming from location rather than pattern recognition.

---

# Research Takeaway

The second Bollinger Band filter reinforces a theme that has repeatedly appeared throughout the project:

> Location appears to matter more than pattern recognition.

Daily Structure produced stronger win rates with fewer signals, while Double Bottom / Top produced only modest improvements despite preserving far more opportunities.

At this stage of the research, Daily Structure remains the leading explanation for the improvement in EURUSD mean-reversion performance.
