# Preliminary Conclusion

The Double Bottom / Double Top filter produced the first genuinely respectable NZDUSD Bollinger Band result.

Strengths:

* Large sample size
* Consistent bearish performance
* Win rates above 50% across all horizons

Weaknesses:

* Bullish side deteriorated over time
* Average returns remain small
* Improvement remains modest rather than dramatic

---

# Research Takeaway

This study reinforces an important project lesson:

> **A pattern should be judged by both its statistical strength and its opportunity count.**

Unlike many previous BB filters, the NZDUSD Double Bottom / Double Top study maintained a relatively large sample while producing one of the stronger win-rate profiles in the chapter.

However, the edge remains in the mid-50% range rather than representing a major market inefficiency.
