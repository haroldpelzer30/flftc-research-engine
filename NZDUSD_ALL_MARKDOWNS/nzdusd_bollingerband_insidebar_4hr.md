# Preliminary Conclusion

The Inside Bar filter failed to validate the emerging bearish Double Top hypothesis.

While the previous Double Top study suggested the possibility of a small bearish mean-reversion tendency, the Inside Bar study failed to reproduce that result.

The evidence currently suggests:

* The Double Top result may still be valid.
* The Inside Bar filter does not appear to strengthen it.
* Additional confirmation from the Doji study is needed.

---

# Research Takeaway

This study reinforces one of the strongest themes of the entire project:

> **A filter should be judged not only by how much it improves win rate, but also by how many opportunities it destroys.**

The Inside Bar filter eliminated approximately 90% of opportunities while failing to improve performance.

This is another example where additional confirmation reduced scalability without creating a stronger edge.
