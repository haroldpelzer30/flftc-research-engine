# Preliminary Conclusion

The Doji study is the first independent confirmation of the bearish NZDUSD Bollinger Band hypothesis.

Strengths:

* Consistent bearish performance
* Win rates above 53% at all horizons
* Independent confirmation of the Double Top study

Weaknesses:

* Very small sample size
* Opportunity count reduced by more than 94%
* Scalability concerns remain significant

---

# Research Takeaway

This study provides the strongest evidence so far that NZDUSD may contain a small bearish volatility-expansion inefficiency.

The bearish Doji and bearish Double Top studies arrived at similar conclusions independently, increasing confidence that the signal may represent a genuine market tendency rather than a random statistical fluctuation.

However, the effect remains modest and opportunity frequency is extremely limited.
