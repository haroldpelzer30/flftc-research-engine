# Preliminary Conclusion

The NZDUSD Bollinger Band Alone study looks remarkably similar to the AUDUSD chapter.

Both markets generated:

* Extremely high signal counts
* Very small edges
* Weak average returns
* Results close to random

---

# Research Takeaway

The NZDUSD study provides additional support for an emerging cross-asset theme:

> **Highly correlated markets often produce highly similar research outcomes.**

AUDUSD and NZDUSD generated nearly identical signal counts and nearly identical Bollinger Band performance.

The evidence suggests that market behavior may be more important than indicator selection, reinforcing one of the strongest conclusions of the project so far.
