# Major Findings

## Finding 1

Gold continues to exhibit bullish asymmetry.

Across almost every study:

Bullish > Bearish

This behavior mirrors previous Gold RSI research.

---

## Finding 2

Daily Structure was the strongest filter.

BB Alone:

53.00%
52.07%
51.37%

BB + Daily Structure:

54.29%
57.14%
60.00%

Location dramatically improved results.

---

## Finding 3

Double Bottoms helped.

Inside Bars and Dojis did not.

This reinforces the broader project theme:

Location > Pattern Recognition

---

## Finding 4

Gold appears fundamentally different from most forex markets.

Forex Chapter:

Volatility Alone
≈ Weak

Gold:

Volatility Alone
≈ Useful

Volatility + Location
≈ Meaningfully Better

# Master Report Lessons Reinforced

1. The market matters more than the indicator.

2. Gold exhibits persistent bullish asymmetry.

3. Location appears more informative than pattern recognition.

4. Volatility identifies extremes.
   Location determines whether those extremes matter.

5. Added complexity is not automatically beneficial.
