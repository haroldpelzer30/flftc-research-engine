# Major Findings

## Finding 1

The bullish side repeatedly outperformed the bearish side.

Across multiple independent studies:

Daily Structure → Bullish

Double Bottom → Bullish

Inside Bar → Bullish

---

## Finding 2

The bearish side consistently underperformed.

Nearly every bearish study remained below 50%.

---

## Finding 3

The strongest results were tied to location and structure.

Successful studies involved:

- Previous Daily Lows
- Retests (Double Bottoms)
- Volatility Contraction (Inside Bars)

The Doji pattern failed.

---

## Finding 4

The chapter reinforced a major project theme:

> Location may matter more than pattern recognition.

Daily Structure and Double Bottoms repeatedly outperformed Dojis.

---

## Finding 5

Consistency mattered more than magnitude.

No single study produced an overwhelming edge.

However, multiple independent studies identified the same bullish tendency.
