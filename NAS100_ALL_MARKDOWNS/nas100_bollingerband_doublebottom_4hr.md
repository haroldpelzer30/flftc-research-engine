# Preliminary Conclusion

The Double Bottom filter did not add value to NAS100.

Strengths:

* Bullish edge remains intact.
* Positive longer-term returns.
* Reasonable sample size.

Weaknesses:

* Inferior to BB Alone.
* Fewer opportunities.
* No meaningful improvement.

---

# Research Takeaway

This study provides additional support for the NAS100 thesis:

> The edge appears to come primarily from the market itself rather than the pattern.

The Double Bottom pattern added complexity but did not improve results.

---

# Research Note

> The NAS100 BB + Double Bottom study produced respectable bullish results but failed to outperform the BB Alone baseline. While the pattern maintained the market's bullish bias, it reduced both opportunity count and overall performance. The findings suggest that the NAS100's structural bullish drift remains the primary source of the edge rather than the Double Bottom pattern itself.
