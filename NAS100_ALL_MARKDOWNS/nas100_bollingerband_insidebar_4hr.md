# Preliminary Conclusion

The Inside Bar filter produced outstanding headline statistics but extremely limited opportunity count.

Strengths:

* Highest bullish win rate in the NAS100 chapter.
* Strong average returns.
* Clear bullish bias.

Weaknesses:

* Very small sample size.
* Limited statistical confidence.
* Difficult to scale in real-world trading.

---

# Research Takeaway

This study reinforces a recurring project lesson:

> Higher win rates often come at the cost of dramatically fewer opportunities.

The Inside Bar filter improved headline performance while reducing signal frequency to extremely low levels.

---

# Research Note

> The NAS100 BB + Inside Bar study generated the strongest bullish win rates observed in the NAS100 chapter, including a 72.22% one-candle win rate. However, the result was produced from only 18 bullish signals. While the statistics are impressive, the extremely limited sample size suggests caution when interpreting the findings. The result is best viewed as supporting evidence for the NAS100 bullish drift thesis rather than a standalone trading edge.
