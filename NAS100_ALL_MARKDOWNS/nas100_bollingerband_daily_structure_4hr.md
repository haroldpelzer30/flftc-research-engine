# Preliminary Conclusion

Daily Structure did not meaningfully improve NAS100 performance.

Strengths:

* Strong bullish average returns.
* Consistent bullish bias.
* Supports the broader drift hypothesis.

Weaknesses:

* Extremely small sample size.
* Opportunity count reduced by more than 90%.
* No clear improvement over BB Alone.

---

# Research Takeaway

This study reinforces one of the strongest lessons from the index chapter:

> Don't over-filter a market that already wants to go up.

The NAS100 appears capable of generating strong Bollinger Band results without requiring additional location-based filters.

---

# Research Note

> The NAS100 BB + Daily Structure study produced respectable results but failed to materially improve upon the already-strong BB Alone baseline. More than 90% of opportunities were eliminated while overall performance remained broadly similar. The findings suggest that NAS100's structural bullish drift may already contain most of the edge, reducing the value of additional filtering.
