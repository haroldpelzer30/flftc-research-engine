This is one of the strongest BB Alone results observed in the entire project.

Weaknesses:


# Research Note

> The NAS100 BB Alone study represents the strongest evidence yet that Bollinger Bands become increasingly effective in markets with persistent bullish drift. Lower-band volatility extremes produced win rates exceeding 60% over the 6-candle horizon while maintaining a large sample size. The study strongly supports the hypothesis that market structure and long-term directional bias play a major role in determining the usefulness of volatility-based signals.
