# Preliminary Conclusion

The Inside Bar filter was a complete failure on USDCAD.

Strengths:

* None clearly identifiable.

Weaknesses:

* Extremely low signal count.
* No consistent edge.
* Worse than BB Alone.
* Worse than BB + Daily Structure.
* Worse than BB + Double Bottom / Top.

---

# Research Takeaway

This study provides one of the strongest examples yet of a recurring project lesson:

> **A filter can destroy nearly all opportunities while adding absolutely no predictive value.**

The Inside Bar filter reduced opportunities by more than 90% and produced results that were effectively random or worse.
