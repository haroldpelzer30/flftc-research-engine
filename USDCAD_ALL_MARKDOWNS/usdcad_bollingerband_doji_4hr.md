---

# Research Takeaway

This study reinforces another major project lesson:

> **Extremely selective filters can create impressive-looking percentages that are supported by too few opportunities to be practically meaningful.**

A 55% win rate on:

```text
20 bearish signals
```

is far less compelling than a 54% win rate on hundreds or thousands of opportunities.
