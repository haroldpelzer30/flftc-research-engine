# Research Takeaway

This study reinforces another major project lesson:

> **Fewer opportunities do not automatically imply a stronger edge, but some markets appear to contain higher-quality opportunities than others.**

USDCAD generated only:

```text
596 signals
```

yet outperformed many currencies that generated:

```text
4000+
signals
```

The market itself appears to matter more than the indicator used to measure it.
