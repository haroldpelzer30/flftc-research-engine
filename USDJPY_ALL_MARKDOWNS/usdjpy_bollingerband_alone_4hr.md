# Preliminary Conclusion

The Ninja appears to be continuing its long-standing project reputation:

```text
Lots of opportunities
Very little edge
```

Strengths:

* Massive sample size
* Consistent data

Weaknesses:

* Edge decays rapidly
* Bearish side fails completely
* Negative average returns
* No evidence of a durable inefficiency

---

# Research Takeaway

> **USDJPY continues to be one of the strongest examples that an indicator is not automatically an edge.**

Both RSI and Bollinger Bands generated thousands of opportunities on the Ninja, yet neither produced the type of persistent inefficiency observed in stronger markets such as USDCAD, USDCHF, Gold, or the major indices.
