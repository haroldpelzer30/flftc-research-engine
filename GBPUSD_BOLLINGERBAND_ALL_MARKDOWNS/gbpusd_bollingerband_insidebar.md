# Preliminary Conclusion

Inside Bars generated the highest headline win rate tested so far on GBPUSD.

However:

* Nearly 90% of opportunities were removed.
* The edge largely vanished after the first candle.
* Average returns remain unimpressive.

---

# Research Takeaway

The GBPUSD Inside Bar study reinforces a recurring theme of the project:

> A higher win rate is not automatically a better edge if it comes at the cost of most opportunities.

Inside Bars improved the headline statistic but dramatically reduced trade frequency, raising the question of whether the improvement justifies the loss of scalability.
