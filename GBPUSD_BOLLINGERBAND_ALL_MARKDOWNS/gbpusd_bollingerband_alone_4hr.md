# Key Findings

## Observation 1

GBPUSD generated nearly the same opportunity count as EURUSD.

| Asset | Total Signals |
|---------|---------:|
| EURUSD | 5,274 |
| GBPUSD | 4,675 |

---

## Observation 2

GBPUSD displayed a small short-term reversal tendency.

Both sides produced:

* ~52% after 1 candle

However, the edge faded rapidly.

---

## Observation 3

Performance regressed toward randomness.

By 6 candles:

* Bullish: 49.46%
* Bearish: 49.62%

Both sides were essentially random.

---

## Observation 4

Average returns remained weak.

Returns hovered around zero and became slightly negative as holding periods increased.

---

# Preliminary Conclusion

GBPUSD Bollinger Band Alone performed slightly better than EURUSD Bollinger Band Alone.

However:

* The improvement was small.
* The edge was short-lived.
* The signal quality deteriorated quickly over time.
