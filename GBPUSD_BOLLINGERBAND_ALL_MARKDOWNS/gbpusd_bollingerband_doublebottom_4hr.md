# Preliminary Conclusion

Double Bottom / Top improved Bollinger Band signals modestly.

However:

* The improvement was small.
* Most gains vanished by the 3-candle horizon.
* Average returns suggest little practical edge.

---

# Research Takeaway

The GBPUSD Double Bottom / Top study reinforces a recurring project theme:

> **A filter can improve a statistic without creating an edge.**

While Double Bottoms and Double Tops improved headline win rates slightly, they failed to produce the sustained improvement seen with Daily Structure.

At this stage, Daily Structure remains the dominant Bollinger Band filter on GBPUSD.
