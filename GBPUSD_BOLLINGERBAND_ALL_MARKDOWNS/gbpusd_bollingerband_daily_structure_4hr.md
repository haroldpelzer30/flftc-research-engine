# Key Findings

## Observation 1

Daily Structure dramatically improved Bollinger Band performance.

BB Alone:

* ~52% at best
* Quickly regressed toward randomness

BB + Daily Structure:

* 55%–56% range
* Stronger and more stable edge

---

## Observation 2

GBPUSD again exhibits bearish asymmetry.

Bearish Side:

* 52.83%
* 56.33%
* 53.64%

Bullish Side:

* 54.87%
* 54.60%
* 50.14%

The bearish side remains stronger, especially at the 3-candle horizon.

---

## Observation 3

Daily Structure reduced opportunities substantially.

| Test | Signals |
|---------|---------:|
| BB Alone | 4,675 |
| BB + Daily Structure | 730 |

Opportunity Reduction:

**~84.4%**

---

## Observation 4

The bearish average returns support the win-rate findings.

Bearish signals produced:

* Negative returns at every horizon

This supports the idea that GBPUSD reversals from Daily Structure highs contain meaningful predictive information.

# Preliminary Conclusion

Daily Structure once again proved to be the strongest Bollinger Band filter tested.

The pattern observed on EURUSD has now repeated on GBPUSD:

* BB Alone = weak
* BB + Daily Structure = strong
* Bearish side stronger than bullish side

---

# Research Takeaway

The GBPUSD Daily Structure study strengthens one of the strongest emerging conclusions of the project:

> **Location appears to matter more than the indicator itself.**

Both EURUSD and GBPUSD produced their strongest Bollinger Band results when Daily Structure was added.

The market appears to care far more about where volatility expansion occurs than the volatility expansion itself.
