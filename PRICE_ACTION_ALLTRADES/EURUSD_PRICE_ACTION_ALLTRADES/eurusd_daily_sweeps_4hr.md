## Observation 5

Daily sweeps alone do not appear to create a strong edge in EURUSD.

The market sweeps previous daily highs and lows frequently, but most of those events do not lead to meaningful reversals.

# Preliminary Conclusion

The Daily Sweep concept appears too broad.

Strengths:

* Massive sample size.
* Consistent results.
* Easy to identify.

Weaknesses:

* Very weak edge.
* Little directional advantage.
* Average returns are minimal.

---

# Research Takeaway

This is an important result because it challenges a common trading belief.

Many traders assume:

```text
Daily Sweep
=
Automatic Reversal
```

The data suggests otherwise.

Most sweeps appear to be:

```text
Normal Market Behavior
```

rather than high-quality reversal opportunities.

---

# Research Note

> The EURUSD Daily Sweep study generated one of the largest samples in the entire project, with 9,288 total signals. Despite the enormous sample size, win rates remained near 51% on both sides. The findings suggest that sweeping a previous daily high or low alone is insufficient to create a meaningful trading edge. Additional context, filters, or location factors may be required to separate meaningful liquidity events from routine market noise.
