# Preliminary Conclusion

Daily Structure clearly improved the AUDUSD Bollinger Band results.

However:

* Most of the improvement occurs immediately after the signal.
* The edge deteriorates rapidly over time.
* Opportunity count was reduced by nearly 89%.

The evidence suggests Daily Structure may identify short-term reactions rather than durable reversals.

---

# Research Takeaway

The AUDUSD Daily Structure study reinforces another emerging project theme:

> **Location may improve a signal's timing without necessarily improving its long-term predictive power.**

While Daily Structure improved the headline win rate, the advantage largely disappeared by the 6-candle horizon.

This suggests Daily Structure may be better at identifying short-term bounces than sustained trend reversals.
