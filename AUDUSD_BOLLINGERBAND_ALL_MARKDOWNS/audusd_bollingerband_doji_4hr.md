# Preliminary Conclusion

The Doji filter produced one of the strongest headline win rates in the AUDUSD BB chapter.

However:

* Opportunity count collapsed by nearly 95%.
* The bullish side showed no edge.
* The bearish side's edge faded quickly.
* Average returns remained weak.

The result appears more consistent with a short-term bounce than a meaningful reversal signal.

---

# Research Takeaway

This study reinforces another recurring project lesson:

> **A filter can create an attractive headline statistic while simultaneously destroying most of the opportunities.**

The AUDUSD Doji study improved the headline win rate but reduced signal count from over 4,600 opportunities to only 245.

The evidence suggests that Dojis may identify temporary exhaustion points, but they do not appear to create a durable forecasting advantage on AUDUSD.
