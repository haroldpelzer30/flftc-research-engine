# Preliminary Conclusion

Inside Bars produced the most interesting AUDUSD BB result so far.

However:

* Signal count collapsed by over 92%.
* The bullish edge remained modest.
* The bearish side showed no meaningful predictive power.

This result appears stronger than BB Alone and Double Bottoms, but remains weaker than the best RSI studies.

---

# Research Takeaway

The AUDUSD Inside Bar study highlights an important lesson:

> **A filter can improve one side of a market while simultaneously degrading the other.**

The bullish side improved gradually with time, while the bearish side failed entirely.

This reinforces the importance of evaluating bullish and bearish edges independently rather than assuming symmetry.
