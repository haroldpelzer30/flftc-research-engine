## Observation 1

Double Bottoms and Double Tops occurred frequently on AUDUSD.

```text
2170 signals
```

### BB Alone

```text

51.16%
```

# Preliminary Conclusion

The Double Bottom / Double Top filter failed to improve the underlying Bollinger Band signal.

Despite producing over:

```text
2000+
signals
```

the results remained near random and in some cases deteriorated below the BB Alone baseline.

---

# Research Takeaway

This study reinforces a growing project theme:

> **Pattern recognition often describes what price has already done rather than predicting what price will do next.**

AUDUSD generated a large number of Double Bottom and Double Top formations, but those formations failed to produce a meaningful forecasting advantage.

The evidence suggests that pattern frequency alone is not evidence of predictive power.
