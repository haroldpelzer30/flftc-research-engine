# Major Findings

### Finding 1

AUDUSD generated a large number of RSI opportunities:

* 3,487 total RSI signals

However, the resulting edge remained relatively modest.

---

### Finding 2

Daily Structure produced the only meaningful improvement observed.

Even then:

* Opportunity count fell substantially.
* Win-rate improvement was only a few percentage points.

---

### Finding 3

Pattern-based filters performed poorly.

This includes:

* Double Bottom / Top
* Inside Bar

These filters generally reduced opportunities far more than they improved edge quality.

---

### Finding 4

Weekly Structure performed particularly poorly.

This supports the working hypothesis that Weekly Structure may be too distant and too coarse to provide meaningful context for H4 RSI reversals.

---

# AUDUSD Conclusion

AUDUSD exhibited frequent RSI extremes but relatively weak mean-reversion behavior.

The strongest result observed was:

**RSI + Daily Structure (Bullish): 55.02%**

Overall, AUDUSD reinforces several emerging project themes:

* Opportunity count does not equal edge quality.
* Most filters reduce opportunities more than they improve win rates.
* Location appears more important than pattern recognition.
* Weekly Structure may not be meaningful context for H4 mean-reversion studies.
