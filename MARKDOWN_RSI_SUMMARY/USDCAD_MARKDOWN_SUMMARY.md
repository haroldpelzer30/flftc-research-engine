# USDCAD H4 RSI Research Summary

## Executive Summary

USDCAD emerged as one of the strongest and most unique assets in the entire forex research project.

Unlike most currency pairs, USDCAD displayed a persistent bullish mean-reversion tendency across multiple studies while the bearish side consistently underperformed.

The research repeatedly demonstrated that:

* Bullish reversals strengthened over longer holding periods.
* Bearish reversals frequently failed.
* The strongest results were highly selective.
* Weekly Structure weakened performance.
* Double Bottom confirmation produced the strongest forex result observed so far.

# Major Findings

## 1. Strong Bullish Drift

Every completed study showed stronger bullish performance than bearish performance.

Bullish reversals consistently outperformed bearish reversals.

This is one of the clearest examples in the project that:

> Long and short edges are often not symmetrical.

---

## 2. Selective Opportunities Produced Strong Results

USDCAD generated relatively few opportunities compared to:

* GBPUSD
* AUDUSD
* NZDUSD
* USDJPY

Yet produced some of the strongest win rates observed.

This supports the project hypothesis:

> The strongest market inefficiencies may emerge when opportunities are selective.

---

## 3. Double Bottom Was One of the Few Successful Filters

Unlike many other assets, the Double Bottom filter improved results while preserving a reasonable number of opportunities.

```text
RSI Alone:          63.87%
Double Bottom:      66.08%
```

This suggests that some filters may be useful on specific assets even if they fail elsewhere.

---

## 4. Weekly Structure Failed Again

Weekly Structure:

* Reduced opportunities dramatically.
* Produced weaker bullish performance.
* Failed to outperform simpler approaches.

This reinforces one of the strongest themes in the project:

> Daily Structure appears more relevant to H4 reversals than Weekly Structure.

---

# Final Assessment

USDCAD is one of the strongest and most unique assets tested in the forex universe.

Key characteristics:

* Strong bullish mean-reversion tendencies.
* Weak bearish mean-reversion tendencies.
* Consistent improvement over longer holding periods.
* Relatively low opportunity count.
* Strong evidence of asset-specific behavior.

The research suggests that USDCAD's edge is not simply the result of RSI.

Rather, the market itself appears to possess a persistent bullish reversal characteristic that repeatedly emerges under oversold conditions.

# Final Conclusion

USDCAD stands alongside Gold and USDCHF as one of the most compelling assets studied so far.

Its defining characteristic is not merely a high win rate, but a remarkably persistent bullish mean-reversion tendency that survives across multiple research frameworks.

The evidence suggests that USDCAD is a market where:

> Oversold conditions frequently lead to meaningful bullish reversals, while overbought conditions do not produce equivalent bearish reversals.
