# Preliminary Conclusion

The Inside Bar filter did not help Gold.

Strengths:

* Decent sample size.
* Strong short-term reaction.

Weaknesses:

* Edge collapses quickly.
* Negative longer-horizon returns.
* Underperforms Daily Structure and Double Bottom significantly.

# Research Takeaway

This study reinforces an emerging project theme:

> A signal that predicts a short-term bounce is not necessarily a signal that predicts a meaningful reversal.

The Inside Bar appears to identify:

```text
Short-Term Exhaustion
```
