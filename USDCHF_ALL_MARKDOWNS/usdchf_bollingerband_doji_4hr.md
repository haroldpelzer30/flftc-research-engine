# Preliminary Conclusion

* Only 25 bullish signals exist.

---

# Research Takeaway

The USDCHF Doji study highlights an important tension in quantitative research:

> **The most impressive statistics are often produced by the smallest samples.**

While the 68.00% bullish win rate is remarkable, the extremely limited opportunity count means the result should be treated as an interesting observation rather than a validated market inefficiency.

Further testing across additional assets is required before assigning significance to the finding.
