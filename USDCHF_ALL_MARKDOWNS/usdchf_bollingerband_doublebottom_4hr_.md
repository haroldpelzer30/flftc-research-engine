# Preliminary Conclusion

Double Bottoms provided little additional value on USDCHF.

The filter:

* Reduced opportunities by roughly 52%.
* Failed to improve win rates.
* Did not create a stronger edge than BB Alone.

---

# Research Takeaway

The USDCHF Double Bottom study reinforces another recurring project lesson:

> **A filter can reduce opportunity count without improving edge quality.**

Unlike GBPUSD, where Double Bottoms modestly improved performance, USDCHF gained virtually nothing from the additional pattern filter.

This provides further evidence that the effectiveness of a filter depends heavily on the underlying market rather than the pattern itself.
