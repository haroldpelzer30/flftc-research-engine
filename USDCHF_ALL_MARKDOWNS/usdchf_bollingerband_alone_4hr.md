# Key Findings

## Observation 1

USDCHF produced substantially fewer Bollinger Band signals than EURUSD and GBPUSD.

| Asset | Signals |
|---------|---------:|
| EURUSD | 5,274 |
| GBPUSD | 4,675 |
| USDCHF | 1,248 |

This suggests USDCHF reaches Bollinger Band extremes much less frequently.

---

## Observation 2

Unlike EURUSD and GBPUSD, the edge did not completely disappear.

Bullish Side:

* 52.42%
* 51.66%
* 51.96%

Bearish Side:

* 53.75%
* 49.66%
* 53.75%

The bearish side remained surprisingly stable.

---

## Observation 3

USDCHF again displays stronger bearish behavior.

Best Result:

* Upper Band Reversal
* 53.75%
* 586 signals

This mirrors the RSI chapter, where USDCHF produced some of the strongest bearish mean-reversion results in the entire project.

# Research Takeaway

USDCHF continues to support one of the strongest conclusions of the project:

> **The market matters more than the indicator.**

The same asset that produced one of the strongest RSI Alone results has now produced the strongest Bollinger Band Alone result.

This suggests USDCHF may possess underlying mean-reverting characteristics that multiple indicators are detecting.
