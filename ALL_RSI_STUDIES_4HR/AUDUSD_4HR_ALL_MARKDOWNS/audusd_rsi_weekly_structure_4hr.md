# Key Findings

### 1. Weekly Structure Performed Poorly

Neither the bullish nor bearish side produced a meaningful edge.

Best result:

* Bearish
* 1 Candle Hold
* 51.26%

All other results were near or below random expectation.

---

### 2. Long-Term Performance Deteriorated

The 6-candle results were particularly weak:

* Bullish: 46.46%
* Bearish: 43.28%

These were among the weakest AUDUSD results observed.

---

### 3. Large Reduction in Opportunities

Signal count declined from:

* 3,487 RSI Alone signals

to:

* 436 Weekly Structure signals

representing an approximately 87% reduction in opportunities.

---

# Research Interpretation

Weekly Structure failed to improve AUDUSD RSI signals.

Unlike Daily Structure, which produced a modest improvement on the bullish side, Weekly Structure significantly reduced signal count while producing weaker overall performance.

This suggests that previous weekly highs and lows may not provide meaningful reversal context for AUDUSD RSI extremes.

---

# Preliminary Conclusion

AUDUSD H4 RSI + Weekly Structure ranks as the weakest AUDUSD filter tested so far.

Final AUDUSD ranking:

1. RSI + Daily Structure (Bullish) — 55.02%
2. RSI + Inside Bar — 53.97%
3. RSI Alone — 52.10%
4. RSI + Double Bottom / Top — 51.66%
5. RSI + Weekly Structure — 51.26%

The evidence from AUDUSD strongly supports several emerging project themes:

* Most filters reduce opportunities more than they improve edge.
* Location-based filters appear more useful than pattern-based filters.
* Weekly Structure did not provide meaningful predictive value for AUDUSD RSI signals.
