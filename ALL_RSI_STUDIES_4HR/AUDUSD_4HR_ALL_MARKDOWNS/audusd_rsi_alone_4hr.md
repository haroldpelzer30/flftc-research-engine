# Research Interpretation

AUDUSD appears similar to GBPUSD in that it generates a large number of RSI opportunities. However, the reversal edge is noticeably weaker than USDCHF and significantly weaker than Gold.

The data suggests that AUDUSD RSI extremes occur frequently but do not consistently lead to strong reversals.

This result provides additional support for one of the project's major discoveries:

> Market inefficiencies appear to be strongest when opportunities are selective, not when they are abundant.

AUDUSD generated thousands of opportunities, but the resulting edge remained relatively small.
