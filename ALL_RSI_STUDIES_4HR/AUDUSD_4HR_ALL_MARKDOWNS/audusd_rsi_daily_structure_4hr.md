# Key Findings

### 1. Daily Structure Improved Bullish Performance

Bullish win rates improved across all holding periods.

Most notable result:

* 6 Candle Hold
* 55.02%
* 309 Signals

This is materially better than the 52.10% achieved by AUDUSD RSI Alone.

---

### 2. Daily Structure Hurt Bearish Performance

The bearish side deteriorated significantly.

Results fell to:

* 46.03% at 3 candles
* 46.03% at 6 candles

These results are below random expectation and suggest that overbought conditions near previous daily highs do not reliably produce bearish reversals in AUDUSD.

---

### 3. Asymmetry Emerged

Unlike USDCHF, which showed relatively balanced bullish and bearish behavior, AUDUSD appears directional.

Bullish setups near previous daily lows performed noticeably better than bearish setups near previous daily highs.

---

# Research Interpretation

AUDUSD appears to respond differently depending on trade direction.

Adding daily structure created a meaningful improvement for bullish setups while simultaneously degrading bearish setups.

This suggests that AUDUSD may possess a bullish mean-reversion tendency near previous daily lows, but little evidence of a reliable bearish reversal tendency near previous daily highs.

---

# Preliminary Conclusion

AUDUSD H4 RSI + Daily Structure is the strongest AUDUSD result observed so far on the bullish side.

However, the bearish side failed to validate.

Future research should treat bullish and bearish AUDUSD setups separately rather than assuming symmetry between long and short signals.
