---

### 2. Minimal Improvement

Best result observed:

* Bearish
* 3-Candle Hold
* 53.97%

While slightly better than RSI Alone, the improvement was modest relative to the large reduction in signal count.

---

### 3. Consistent With Previous Pattern Studies

The Inside Bar filter behaved similarly to the Double Bottom / Double Top filter:

* Fewer opportunities
* Small changes in win rate
* No major enhancement in edge quality

---

# Research Interpretation

The Inside Bar pattern does not appear to materially improve AUDUSD RSI signals.

Although a small increase was observed in one bearish holding period, the improvement was not large enough to justify the substantial reduction in trade opportunities.

This result continues a recurring theme across the project:

> Pattern-based filters frequently reduce signal count more than they improve predictive performance.

---

# Preliminary Conclusion

AUDUSD H4 RSI + Inside Bar does not demonstrate a meaningful advantage over RSI Alone.

Current AUDUSD ranking:

1. RSI + Daily Structure (Bullish) — 55.02%
2. RSI + Inside Bar — 53.97%
3. RSI Alone — 52.10%
4. RSI + Double Bottom / Top — 51.66%
