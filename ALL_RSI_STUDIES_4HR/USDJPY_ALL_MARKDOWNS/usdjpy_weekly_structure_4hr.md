---

# Research Interpretation

Weekly Structure performed better on USDJPY than it did on AUDUSD and NZDUSD.

However:

* Signal count collapsed by roughly 85%.
* Improvements were inconsistent.
* Performance varied dramatically across horizons.

The results do not provide strong evidence that Weekly Structure creates a robust edge.

The study suggests Weekly Structure may occasionally improve specific USDJPY scenarios, but the evidence remains inconsistent and substantially weaker than the strongest findings observed elsewhere in the project.

USDJPY continues to support the broader conclusion that:

* Long and short edges are often not symmetrical.
* Consistency across holding periods may be more important than a single impressive statistic.
