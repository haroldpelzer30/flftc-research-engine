# Research Interpretation

The Double Bottom filter appears to have identified a subset of oversold USDJPY signals that performed slightly better than the RSI Alone baseline.

However:

* Improvement remained modest.
* Opportunity count declined substantially.
* Bearish performance remained weak.

This suggests that Double Bottoms may add some value on the bullish side of USDJPY, but do not transform the strategy into a high-edge system.
