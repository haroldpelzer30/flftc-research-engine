### Holding Period Analysis

* Longer holding periods consistently outperformed shorter holding periods.
* The 6-candle horizon produced the strongest results on both the bullish and bearish sides.

### Average Return Analysis

* Despite win rates above 50%, average returns remained negative.
* This suggests winning trades occur slightly more often than losing trades, but losing moves tend to be larger than winning moves.
