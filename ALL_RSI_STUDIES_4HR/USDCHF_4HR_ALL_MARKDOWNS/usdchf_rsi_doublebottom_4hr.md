# Key Findings

### Observation 1

Double Top / Double Bottom preserved a large sample size:

* 506 total signals
* Roughly half the signals of RSI Alone

### Observation 2

Bullish performance remained nearly identical to RSI Alone:

* RSI Alone Bullish: 54.62%
* Double Bottom Bullish: 54.70%

### Observation 3

Bearish performance improved:

* RSI Alone Bearish: 57.00%
* Double Top Bearish: 58.46%

### Observation 4

Double Top / Double Bottom significantly outperformed Daily Structure:

* Higher win rates
* More consistent results
* Better preservation of signal quality

### Observation 5

The strongest result occurred on the bearish side:

* RSI Overbought
* Double Top Confirmation
* 6-Candle Holding Period
* **58.46% Win Rate**

---

# Preliminary Conclusion

Double Top / Double Bottom appears to be a superior filter compared to Daily Structure on USDCHF.

The pattern:

* Reduced signal count
* Preserved the bullish edge
* Improved the bearish edge

This suggests that market structure patterns may be more important than simple proximity to daily highs and lows.
