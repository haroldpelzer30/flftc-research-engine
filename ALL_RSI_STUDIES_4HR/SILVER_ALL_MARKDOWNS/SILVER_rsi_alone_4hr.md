# Key Findings

### Observation 1

Silver displays a strong bullish asymmetry.

* Bullish reversals consistently outperformed bearish reversals.
* The strongest result occurred after oversold conditions.

### Observation 2

The best bullish result occurred at the 3-candle horizon.

* 62.50% win rate
* 48 oversold signals

This is one of the strongest RSI Alone results observed so far.

### Observation 3

Bearish reversals performed poorly.

* 41.81%
* 38.98%
* 40.68%

These results are comparable to NAS100's weak short-side performance.

### Observation 4

Average bullish returns were exceptionally strong.

* 1 Candle: +0.6124%
* 3 Candles: +1.0329%
* 6 Candles: +1.4333%

Returns increased as holding periods increased.

### Observation 5

Average bearish returns remained positive.

Even after overbought signals:

* Silver continued rising on average.
* Longer holding periods produced larger gains.
