# Key Findings

### 1. Strongest Forex RSI-Alone Result Observed

USDCAD produced:

* **63.87%**
* Oversold
* 6-Candle Hold

This is currently the strongest RSI-Alone forex result observed in the project.

---

### 2. Opportunity Count Was Extremely Low

USDCAD generated:

* 238 Oversold Signals
* 246 Overbought Signals
* 484 Total Signals

This is less than half of USDCHF and dramatically lower than every other major currency studied.

---

The bearish side never produced a meaningful edge.

---

### 4. Consistency Improved With Time

Unlike many previous studies, the bullish edge strengthened as additional candles were allowed to develop.

This is the opposite of what was observed in several other forex pairs.

---

# Research Interpretation

USDCAD immediately stands out as one of the most unusual forex assets studied.

Unlike:

* AUDUSD
* NZDUSD
* GBPUSD
* USDJPY

USDCAD generated very few RSI opportunities.

However, the opportunities that did occur produced exceptionally strong bullish reversal performance.

This result provides additional support for the working hypothesis:

> The strongest market inefficiencies may emerge when opportunities are selective, not because they are rare, but because the market conditions that create them are more meaningful.
