# Research Interpretation

USDCAD continues to behave very differently from most major currency pairs.

The strongest results are appearing:

* On the bullish side

This reinforces several project themes:

* Opportunity count does not equal edge quality.
* Selective opportunities may contain stronger inefficiencies.
* Long and short edges should be evaluated separately.
* Strong market behavior can exist even with relatively few signals.
