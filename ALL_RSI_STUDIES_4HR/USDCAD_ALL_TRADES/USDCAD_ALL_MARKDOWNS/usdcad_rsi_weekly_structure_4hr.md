# Research Interpretation

This study reinforces one of the strongest emerging conclusions of the entire project:

> Weekly Structure may not be meaningful context for H4 RSI reversals.

The Weekly Structure filter:

* Reduced opportunities dramatically.
* Failed to improve bullish performance.
* Produced weaker results than Daily Structure.
* Produced weaker results than Double Bottom confirmation.

The evidence continues to suggest that weekly highs and lows may be too distant and too coarse to provide useful information for a strategy with a typical holding period of 12–24 hours.

# Current Conclusion

USDCAD remains one of the strongest and most unique assets studied in the forex universe.

However, Weekly Structure once again failed to justify itself.

The study provides additional evidence that:

* Daily Structure is more relevant than Weekly Structure.
* Nearby market context appears more useful than distant market context.
* Weekly Structure often destroys opportunities without providing meaningful predictive improvement.

USDCAD's strongest findings continue to come from:

* RSI Alone
* RSI + Daily Structure
* RSI + Double Bottom

while Weekly Structure joins a growing list of disappointing filters across multiple assets.
