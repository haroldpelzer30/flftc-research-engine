# Research Interpretation

USDCAD continues to exhibit a remarkably persistent bullish mean-reversion tendency.

Across both studies completed so far:

### RSI Alone

```text
63.87%
```

### RSI + Daily Structure

```text
64.71%
```

the same pattern emerges:

* Bullish reversals strengthen over time.
* Bearish reversals weaken over time.
* Positive average returns increase as holding periods extend.

This suggests the bullish edge may be driven by underlying market behavior rather than by the Daily Structure filter itself.

---

# Preliminary Conclusion

USDCAD remains one of the most unique assets studied so far.

The evidence currently suggests:

* Strong bullish mean-reversion tendencies.
* Weak bearish mean-reversion tendencies.
* Increasing edge strength as holding periods increase.
* Daily Structure provides only marginal improvement over RSI Alone.

The most important observation from this study is that:

> The bullish drift continues.

Every completed USDCAD study has shown stronger bullish performance than bearish performance, reinforcing the hypothesis that USDCAD possesses a market-specific bullish reversal characteristic not observed in most other forex pairs.
