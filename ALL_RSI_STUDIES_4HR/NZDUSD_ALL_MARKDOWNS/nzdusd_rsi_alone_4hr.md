---

### 2. Weak Mean-Reversion Edge

Best result observed:

* Overbought
* 3-Candle Hold
* 52.86%

All other results clustered near 50-53%.

---

### 3. No Evidence of USDCHF-Like Behavior

USDCHF RSI Alone achieved:

* 54.62% bullish
* 57.00% bearish

NZDUSD failed to approach those levels despite generating over three times as many opportunities.

---

# Research Interpretation

NZDUSD appears very similar to AUDUSD.

Both assets:

* Generate frequent RSI extremes.
* Produce large numbers of opportunities.
* Exhibit relatively weak mean-reversion characteristics.
* Fail to demonstrate the stronger edge observed in USDCHF.

This strengthens the project's growing observation that:

> Opportunity frequency alone is not a proxy for edge quality.
