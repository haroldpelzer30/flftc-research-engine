### Observations

- Inside bar compression modestly improved Gold RSI bullish performance versus RSI alone.
- Bullish 3-candle performance reached 59.60%.

- Signal count remained relatively healthy at 250 bullish signals.
- Bearish reversals again deteriorated over longer horizons.
- Gold continued displaying persistent bullish asymmetry characteristics.

---

### Conclusions

- Inside bars may provide short-term stabilization/compression confirmation.
- Compression behavior alone appears weaker than structure-based confirmation.
- Inside bars improved timing modestly but did not outperform:
    - daily structure
    - monthly structure
    - double bottom logic
- Structure-based filters continue to dominate indicator-only or pattern-only filters.
