### Observations

- Gold behaves VERY differently than EURUSD.
- Oversold RSI conditions showed significantly stronger bullish reversal behavior.
- Best result so far:
    - 57.10% bullish reversal over 3 candles.
- Bearish reversals were weak and deteriorated over longer holding periods.
- Gold appears to maintain bullish drift characteristics even after overbought conditions.

---

### Conclusions

- Gold may be much more responsive to RSI exhaustion than EURUSD.
- Bullish oversold conditions appear materially more promising than bearish overbought conditions.
- Structure filters may improve this further.
