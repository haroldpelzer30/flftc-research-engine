### Observation 3

### Observation 4

* Daily Structure Best Result: 56.25%
* Inside Bar Best Result: 54.79%

### Observation 5

Inside Bar compression appears more useful on the bullish side than the bearish side.

---

# Preliminary Conclusion

Inside Bars improved bullish reversal performance on EURUSD, but not enough to surpass Daily Structure.

The filter dramatically reduced signal count while producing only a moderate increase in win rate.

This suggests:

* Compression behavior may contain some information.
* However, that information appears weaker than Daily Structure.
* EURUSD continues to show greater responsiveness to location than to pattern-based confirmation.

# Key Insight

Daily Structure remains the strongest EURUSD filter tested so far.

While Inside Bars improved performance over RSI Alone, they required a substantial reduction in opportunity count and still failed to match the edge produced by Daily Structure.

Current evidence suggests that EURUSD responds more strongly to location-based context than to pattern-recognition filters such as Double Bottoms, Double Tops, and Inside Bars.

---

# Research Takeaway

EURUSD is becoming one of the strongest examples in the project that:

> Location may matter more than pattern recognition.

Daily Structure remains the leader despite producing fewer opportunities, while both Double Bottoms and Inside Bars have failed to generate a comparable improvement in predictive power.
